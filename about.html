About the project

A slower way to see art.

Painted turns familiar masterpieces into intricate digital painting-by-numbers canvases, made to be completed online rather than downloaded.

The idea

Look closely

Working through 120 colours makes the small shifts in light, shadow and texture easier to notice. The aim is less about finishing quickly and more about spending time with the image.

Privacy

Kept in your browser

Painted has no accounts and uploads no painting progress. It stores only the completed connected areas in your browser’s local storage.

The collection
