How to paint

There is no timer and no score. Work through all 120 colours in any order you like.

  1. 01

    Choose a masterpiece

    Open the Gallery and select any painting. Each artwork has its own page and remembers its own progress.

  2. 02

    Select a colour

    Choose one of the 120 numbered swatches in the paintbox. On a phone, tap the Colour button to open it; choosing a swatch returns you to the canvas.

  3. 03

    Find the matching number

    Click an area carrying the same number. Only that connected area is filled; other areas with the same number remain for you to find.

  4. 04

    Use the tools when needed

    Highlight Number reveals the selected regions and Show Original offers a reference. Scroll over the painting to zoom on desktop, or pinch and drag it on a phone. You can zoom up to 500% to reveal numbers in the smallest areas.

  5. 05

    Leave and return

    Your completed connected areas are saved automatically in this browser. No account or download is required.
